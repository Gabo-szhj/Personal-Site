Surgical VR System

Project Info:

This is a presentation deck to pitch a developing patent that can improve the safety and efficiency of surgical practices. This VR system can allow the surgeon to view the patient from different angles, cast a pre-clinical screening on the patient and provide real-time help from other experts.
