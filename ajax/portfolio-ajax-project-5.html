Water Filtration System - Kidogo

Project Info:

This is a recommendation deck for the Kidogo Foundation to expand the size of the early child education centers in the Nairobi slum in Kenya. The recommendation involves the distribution of a water filtration system which can improve the quality of life and act as a fundraiser for the Kidogo Foundation.
