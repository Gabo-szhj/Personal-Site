City Scale Decarbonization and Duck Curve Explained

Project Info:

This is the explanation video of the city scale decarbonization experiment conducted by Stanford. This experiment took advantage of the duck curve created by the solar energy to heat and cool the campus with minimal carbon dioxide produced.
