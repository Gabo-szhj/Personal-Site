About Me

Get To Know Me

Billions are living with deadly consequence of environmental pollution,

I will change that.

At age 15, in front of two hundred students at a local middle school, I took a deep breath and gave talk about nuclear fusion as the energy source of the future.

At age 16, I dove straight down the rabbit hole of environmental sustainability. Refusing to leave local waste problems for others to solve, a group of friends and I built Faunas.Co, a company dedicated to closing the circular economy loop in suburban Las Vegas.

Now, as an undergraduate at Caltech, I am focusing on gathering all the tools I need to actualize my purpose:

Solving waste pollution problems in third-world countries, through developing sustainable energy hardware, innovating biodegradable semiconductors, streamlining production through machine learning, and seeking the economic incentives behind sustainable development.

I pursued what I loved, not what I was supposed to do. Even if I am the only girl speaking at the conference, standing before big company executives, and nerding out about energy hardware, I am not afraid to keep going.

What I Do?

Sustainability Research and Blog

In 2019 I fell in love with advocating for renewable energy sources to power our world through my personal blog on Medium.com. Subsequently, I patented biodegradable nanocomposite for semiconductor applications with Stony Brook University and published ethics of sustainable development article on peer-reviewed journal. My quest continues…

Business Consulting

Companies often look for a fresh view of their most disturbing issues. My team and I offered consulting recommendations through pitch decks for a variety of organizations: from Born This Way Foundation (a teen mental health awareness non-profit) to X-Prize Foundation (an innovation-based competition host).

Model Air Plane

I am the 2023 Science Olympiad National Model Air Plane Champion. Making such a fragile mechanical system is such a frustrating yet rewarding experience. Through the 5hr+ testing sessions and multiple failed designs, I got a bittersweet taste of perseverance, teamwork, patience, and eventual success.

Loan Signing Agent

I am a certified notary public in Nevada and California. Furthermore, as a registered US loan signing agent, I am well versed in loan document identification, legal bindings of signatures, and communicating with the clients.

Web Design

Since 2020, I have completed numerous web development and design tasks. My clients range from startups to independent best-selling authors. I am well-versed in html and WordPress utilization. The website you are viewing now is an example of my project!

Machine Learning

I have always believed that machine learning will be the engine of future technological innovation. I have been self-learning machine-learning math and Python programming techniques and completed various projects such as The Breast Cancer Visual Detector and Architectural Energy Efficiency Prediction.
