At age 16, I dove straight down the rabbit hole of environmental sustainability. Fixing the problems in the world that everyone just left for others to solve.

I want to contribute my part in solving air pollution problems in third-world countries, innovate the current house building materials to more sustainable alternatives, and developing new and innovative sustainable nuclear fusion technology.

I am often the only girl speaking in front of the school, building STEM projects, and not in any medical clubs. I love myself as who I am, not anyone else!

What I Do?

Alternative Energy Research

In 2019 I fell in love with the thought of using renewable energy sources to power our cities and the world. I started researching in this field with the focus on nuclear fusion, fuel cells, and wind power.

Pollution Solution Research

Air pollution was such a huge problem where I grew up. Through experiencing the extremes of air pollution to the point of wearing a mask to school every day, I dedicated myself to the investigation the air pollution problem and development of potential solutions.

Biodiversity Monitoring

In summer 2020, I joined a group of like-minded friends for the XPrize rainforest challenge. We developed a potential solution which can monitor biodiversity 24-7 in the rainforests throughout the world.

Business Consulting

For the past two years, I have been involved in many consulting projects for companies and organizations. These projects range anywhere from mental health solution to educating kids in Africa.

Web Design

I first got into the web designing world in the winter of 2020. Some websites I made include Faunas.co for my previous startup project, and the website you are viewing right now!

Hobbies

I enjoy my free time by giving myself the permission to do what I love. Some of my hobbies include figure skating, dancing, music production, acrylic painting, and playing guitar.
